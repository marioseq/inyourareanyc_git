== Description ==
The Twitter Profile section allows you to easily insert Twitter feeds into your website without having to configure/copy/paste any code.

== Features ==
* Display your three most recent Twitter updates
* Place in any Template Area
* Will autosize to the Template Area it is active in
* No need to copy & paste code, simply add your Twitter Username to the page's Meta Settings
